Problem

An important number of children in Lebanon are found in vulnerable communities, and more often than not, these communities are hard to reach and are left without proper educational opportunities.

A solution given to these communities is online e-learning platforms. However, it is a solution for only a small part of children because many communities do not have access to the internet. Furthermore, the content is limited to the material offered by the platform and teachers are not given tools that allow them to tailor the material to their students' needs and make them more relatable to their everyday life.

Solution

DrosBox is a device that allows NGOs and teachers to upload pre-existing digital content as well as create their own, customized and tailored material to their students' needs and customs:

  • User-friendly: DrosBox is powered by Wordpress, a globally-used platform that is customizable and easy to use. It supports more than 120 languages, including Arabic. Making DrosBox a user-friendly device.
  • Interactive: DrosBox uses H5P, a WordPress plugin with the power to turn static boring content into interactive modules backed with an ability to provide instant feedback on children’s answers.
  • Offline Solution: The DrosBox image transforms your Raspberry Pi into a mobile Server that acts like a WIFI hotspot with the ability to run on a simple power bank. Thus, offering hard to reach communities an offline solution.

The combination of these features make DrosBox a unique and strong deployment system at anyone’s disposal around the world. The DrosBox image is available on our website for free and comes pre-loaded with a tutorial and samples to guide the teacher.

What do I have to do to access DrosBox?

Visit [DrosBox.org](http://drosbox.org), download the image and follow the instructions to install it on your device.
