The Problem

Language learning and host country integration

Millions of refugee children settle in host countries where the language of academic instruction is different than their home, creating difficult learning and social barriers within their new school environments. In Lebanon, this issue affects over 300,000 school-aged Syrian refugees, who have come from Syrian public schools predominately taught in Arabic, to Lebanese classrooms where English, Arabic, and French are used to teach core subjects.

With limited in-class and after school training in English and French due to shortened 2nd shift school schedules and no one to practice with at home, where only Arabic is spoken, many refugee learners in Lebanon must learn these foreign languages on their own.

Enter Kindi

Kindi is a mobile application helping refugee learners improve their English through live reading sessions with native speakers from around the world. At the click of a button, a Syrian refugee learner in Lebanon can now read with virtual reading volunteers from the US, Europe and MENA region. During each session, the student and reading volunteer work on pronunciation and comprehensions skills while reading short stories together. With Kindi, refugee learners get someone to practice reading with and volunteers get the chance to make a direct impact in someoneâ€™s life, with both getting some perspective along the way.

How it works

Live demo here.

  1. Refugee learner enters the app, picks a story, and requests a reading buddy.
  2. Reading volunteers are notified when a session request is made and can choose to join the session.
  3. Once in a session, students and volunteers see the same story and are able to interact over audio and on-screen actions like highlighting and note taking.
  4. All session data is stored and shared with teachers (Ex: what words do students mispronounce the most, what does that sound like?)

Big vision

Create a world where every child always has someone to read and learn with, regardless of their location or financial means.
